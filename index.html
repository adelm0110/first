Cantonese Beef

A very simple, quick dish, that is reakky delicious nonetheless. Be sure to buy best-quality beef, and use extremely high heat so the meat quickly ecomes tendeer and does not overcook.

For visual learners, click here.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb beef tenderloin (filet mignon)
  • 1 tb dark soy sauce
  • 2 tsps rice wine
  • 2 tsps cornstarch mixed with 1 tb water
  • 3-4 cups vegetable oil
  • A few smaller slices peeled fresh ginger root, thinly sliced
  • 1 small onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tbs oyster sauce
  • Tomato wedges and coriander (cilantro) sprigs for garnish
  1. Cut the beef into very thin slices about the size if large postage stamps. Put the slices in a bowl, add the soy sauce, rice wine, and cornstarch paste, and mix well. Cover and marinate at room temperature for at least 20-25 minutes.
